Lodging - Etiquette


  1. Do not wear your shoes inside hotels, your Airbnb, ryokan, or any other kind of lodging in Japan.
  2. Do not play any loud music, yell, listen to the TV at a high volume, or do any other activities that may disturb your neighbors. The walls are thin in Japanese homes.
  3. If staying in an Airbnb, be sure to follow the trash separation guide, as Japan is very strict about it.
  4. Take care of your accommodations as if it were your own home: keep it clean and looking presentable.
  5. Follow the rules set by your Airbnb host to avoid paying any additional fees or any other consequence.
  6. Turn off any electronics inside an Airbnb whenever they are not in use to save electricity.
